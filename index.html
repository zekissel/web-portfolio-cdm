About

My name is Zane Kissel and I study Computer Science at the University of Pittsburgh. I've been working on many projects lately, including programming in C++, Java, and HTML/CSS. This site is a collection of projects I have completed for a course offered by Pitt's English dept, Composing Digital Media. The course teaches not only hard skills like image and audio editing software, modern formatting languages, and Github, but it also allows you to learn in a creative fashion by creating your own message at your own pace.
