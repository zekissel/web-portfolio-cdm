Soundscape Narrative




This is my Soundscape Narrative, unofficially titled "Urban vs Nature". For this project, I utilized the software 'Audacity' to create a story using audio alone.

First, I captured audio from the outdoors of Oakland on my phone. Then, I used Audacity to patch together and manipulate the audio so it was one coherent tale. This story describes the juxtaposition seen between the atmospheres and characteristics of urban and nature settings.
